The mindset

Lift heavy. Engineer well. Repeat.

On a personal level, I place a strong emphasis on physical health and long-term discipline. I train consistently and reached a 365-lb bench press through years of focused effort. That same mindset carries into my academic and professional work.

Consistency, patience, and a willingness to push through challenges are what drive results — whether in the gym or in engineering projects. The PR doesn't show up in week three; the senior design project doesn't ship on the first iteration. The gym taught me to keep showing up anyway.
