About · 03

I'm drawn to projects where the engineering doesn't fit cleanly inside one discipline — a control panel that has to talk to a Unity simulation, a carbon-fibre method that swaps a furnace for a microwave, a rocket airframe that has to survive Mach 1.4 and a parachute snap on the same flight.

I prefer to work close to the hardware, instrument the thing properly, and let the data argue back.
